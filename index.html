mumax3

mumax3 is a GPU-accelerated micromagnetic simulation package.

  • Speed-up of up to 100x compared to CPU-based packages.
  • Low memory usage easily allows > 10 million FD cells.

physics:

  • Spin-transfer torque.
  • Simulation window can automatically follow moving domain walls, etc.
  • Define arbitrary exchange between cells: different materials, RKKY coupling etc.
